About the Game

Survival, but make it cozy

Driftwood Camp focuses on comfort and discovery rather than danger.
Players restore forgotten coastal areas, fish near quiet shores, and build useful structures from collected materials.
You actually feel like you're getting somewhere, you know?

How to Play

Explore, collect, restore

Search the Shore

Collect driftwood, shells, tools, and lost cargo.

Build Your Camp

Craft shelters, workstations, and decorations.

Explore Nearby Islands

Travel across different coastal zones.

Manage Resources

Balance food, tools,
and energy.

Expand the Settlement

Unlock new buildings
and activities.
